Safety First

6 Layers of Validation

Insulin errors can be dangerous. Every recommendation passes through graduated safety checks before reaching you.

1

Hard Limits

Absolute boundaries that never flex: max 25U bolus, min 70 mg/dL glucose, max fall rate thresholds.

2

User Confirmation

Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID) required before any insulin delivery. You always have final say.

3

Physiological Bounds

Validates against expected glucose drop, insulin stacking risk, and carb-to-insulin ratios.

4

Predictive Safety

Calculates hypo risk at 15, 30, 60, and 120 minute horizons. Reduces dose if risk detected.

5

Anomaly Detection

Validates sensor data quality, detects compression lows, and flags implausible readings.

6

Learning Validation

Monitors outcomes for algorithm drift and requires minimum confidence before applying learned adjustments.
