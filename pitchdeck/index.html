Key Innovation

Bayesian Learning

Dossi doesn't use hardcoded rules like exercise = +20%

It learns my patterns from actual glucose outcomes.

After each meal:

  1. Observe outcome vs prediction
  2. Update model based on evidence
  3. Build personalized modifiers over time

Why This Approach

Why Bayesian Inference?

The Problem with Rules

  • Hardcoded % don't fit everyone
  • No way to improve over time
  • Can't express uncertainty

Why Bayesian Works

  • Starts with clinical priors
  • Updates beliefs with evidence
  • Knows what it doesn't know

How It Learns

The Learning Loop

After every meal, Dossi observes what actually happened:

1
Predict — "Given this context, glucose should land at X"
2
Observe — "Actual glucose was Y" (higher or lower?)
3
Attribute — "Which factors were active?"
4
Update — "Adjust belief about how that factor affects ME"

Safety First

5 Layers of Validation

Insulin errors can be fatal. Every recommendation passes 5 checks:

1
Hard Limits — Never dose if glucose <70 mg/dL
2
Physiological — Will this drop me too low?
3
Predictive — Hypo risk at 15/30/60/120 min?
4
Anomaly — Is this sensor data trustworthy?
5
Learning — Have recent outcomes been poor?
