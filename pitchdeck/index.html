Safety First

5 Layers of Validation

Insulin errors can be fatal. Every recommendation passes 5 checks:

1Hard Limits ‚Äî Never dose if glucose <70 mg/dL
2Physiological ‚Äî Will this drop me too low?
3Predictive ‚Äî Hypo risk at 15/30/60/120 min?
4Anomaly ‚Äî Is this sensor data trustworthy?
5Learning ‚Äî Have recent outcomes been poor?
