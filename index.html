Intelligent insulin dosing for Type 1 Diabetes

47%

The average person with Type 1 Diabetes spends 47% of their day with glucose levels outside the healthy range. Static pump formulas aren't enough.

Current insulin delivery systems use rigid math that ignores the dozens of factors that actually affect insulin sensitivity. Dossi fills the gap—providing intelligent recommendations that adapt to your life.

How It Works

Dosing that adapts to you

Not generic rules. Personalized recommendations based on your data, your patterns, your physiology.

1

Connect your data

Dossi pulls from Apple Health—CGM readings, exercise, sleep, heart rate variability, and more. Your data stays on your device.

2

Get context-aware recommendations

When you're about to eat, Dossi considers everything: your current glucose, recent exercise, time of day, meal macros, and 35+ other factors.

3

Learn over time

Bayesian algorithms learn your unique patterns. The more you use Dossi, the better it understands how your body responds.

4

Stay safe with 5-layer validation

Every recommendation passes through five safety layers before you see it. Medical-grade guardrails, always active.

Features

Built for real life with T1D

40+ Contextual Factors

Real-time Adaptation

Your sensitivity changes throughout the day. Dossi adjusts its recommendations accordingly—no manual tweaking needed.
